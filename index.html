What age group is this course designed for?

This course is specifically designed for students in Grades 3-5 (ages 8-11). The content and activities are tailored to their developmental stage and learning needs.

How long are the daily sessions?

Each session is 1 hour long, scheduled at convenient times for young learners. We offer multiple time slots to accommodate different schedules.

Who conducts the sessions?

All sessions are conducted by certified child psychologists and experienced educators who specialize in child development and confidence building.

Are the sessions live or pre-recorded?

All sessions are live and interactive, allowing real-time engagement between students and mentors. This ensures personalized attention and immediate feedback.

What if my child misses a session?

We provide session recordings and make-up sessions for missed classes. Our team also offers individual catch-up sessions if needed.

How do you measure progress?

We use a combination of observation, interactive assessments, and parent feedback to track your child's confidence development throughout the course.
